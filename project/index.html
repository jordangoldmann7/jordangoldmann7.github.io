idea

The MLB playoffs consist of 10 teams. All of them are either in the Wild card, Division series, Championship series, or the World series.

Opinion for the Playoffs

The 2017 Major league Playoffs are the most significant in my opinion because the teams that are partaking, are very good. You might think that all teams should be good to got to the playoffs, but this year is different. This years playoffs are significant because you have many teams that are participating as the voted underdog in the playoffs. Then you have the teams that people think would win the whole thing without a doubt. In my opinion, the regular season doesnt matter because the playoffs are a whole other ballgame.

Top teams

The top teams in my opinion are the cleveland indians and the Los Angeles Dodgers because the Indians went to the world series last year and the Dodgers because they were the best team in the league. But of course this doesnt mean anything, these are just my predictions.
